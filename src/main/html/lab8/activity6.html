About Us


We used to serve young people, they like it. Now they've grown old, they still like it, but not the young people though.


Therefore, we have made the decision to exclude young people due to their perceived noise level. We have gone so far as to change the name of the establishment to Kopidiam as a way of expressing our dislike for this particular demographic.


Our Values

Welcome to Kopidiam, the only restaurant in town that values tradition above all else - including basic human decency. We know what you're thinking, "But aren't all restaurants traditional in some way?" Well, not like Kopidiam. We take pride in our strict "no young people" policy, because let's be real, who needs the energy and vibrancy of youth when you can have the sluggish monotony of middle age? Plus, with the absence of young people, we're able to maintain the classic, outdated decor that we all know and love. So come on down to Kopidiam, where the only thing that's not traditional is our blatant ageism. We promise it'll be a dining experience you won't forget - or rather, that you will forget because you'll be too old to remember it.
